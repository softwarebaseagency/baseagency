SECTION 1 - PURPOSE
The purpose of this CRM & Sales Pipeline Tracker is to help Base Agency record, manage, follow up, and convert leads into paying clients.
This tracker shall be used to control all sales activities, including client information, lead source, service requirements, meetings, proposals, quotations, follow-ups, deal status, expected value, closing date, and final sales results.
SECTION 2 - SALES OBJECTIVES
1. Record all potential clients.
2. Track every sales opportunity.
3. Follow up with clients professionally.
4. Know which clients need meetings.
5. Know which clients received proposals or quotations.
6. Track expected sales value.
7. Improve closing rate.
8. Avoid losing clients due to weak follow-up.
9. Prepare weekly sales reports.
10. Build a professional client database.

SECTION 19 - LEAD PRIORITY SYSTEM
High Priority
Client has budget, urgent need, decision-maker available, and clear service requirement.
Medium Priority
Client has interest but needs more time, more information, or internal review.
Low Priority
Client is not ready, no clear budget, or follow-up is required later.
SECTION 20 - FOLLOW-UP RULES
1. Every new lead must be contacted within 24 hours.
2. Every contacted client must receive a next follow-up date.
3. No lead should remain without status.
4. Proposal follow-up must be done within 2 working days.
5. Quotation follow-up must be done within 2 working days.
6. Important client communication must be recorded in the contact log.
7. Lost deals must include the reason lost.
8. Closed deals must be sent to Finance and Operations.
SECTION 21 - SALES HANDOVER TO OPERATIONS
When a client approves a proposal or quotation, Sales must hand over the project to Operations.
Required Handover Information
1. Client name.
2. Contact person.
3. Approved service.
4. Approved scope.
5. Approved price.
6. Payment status.
7. Deadline.
8. Client requirements.
9. Files / materials received.
10. Special notes.

SECTION 27 - FINAL DECLARATION
This CRM & Sales Pipeline Tracker is approved as the official sales tracking and client management system of Base Agency.
It defines lead tracking, sales pipeline stages, client contact logs, meeting tracking, proposal tracking, quotation tracking, closed deal records, lost deal records, daily sales activity, weekly reporting, client target lists, sales handover, dashboard indicators, responsibilities, and sales control rules.
All sales staff, managers, finance representatives, operations representatives, and authorized persons must use this tracker to manage sales activities, client follow-ups, proposals, quotations, approvals, and closed deals.
