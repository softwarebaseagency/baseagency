SECTION 1 - PURPOSE
The purpose of this Finance, Income & Expense Tracker is to help Base Agency record, monitor, review, and control all company financial activities, including income, expenses, invoices, receipts, petty cash, salaries, office rent, supplier payments, client payments, profit, and monthly finance reporting.
This tracker shall be used to support daily finance control and monthly management review.
SECTION 2 - FINANCE OBJECTIVES
1. Record all company income.
2. Record all company expenses.
3. Track client payments.
4. Track unpaid invoices.
5. Track supplier payments.
6. Control petty cash.
7. Monitor monthly salary costs.
8. Monitor office rent and fixed costs.
9. Calculate monthly profit and loss.
10. Prepare monthly finance reports for Management.

SECTION 18 - DAILY FINANCE ROUTINE
1. Record all income received.
2. Record all expenses paid.
3. Save receipts and invoices.
4. Update petty cash balance.
5. Update payment status.
6. Notify Management about urgent finance issues.
SECTION 19 - WEEKLY FINANCE ROUTINE
1. Review income register.
2. Review expense register.
3. Review unpaid invoices.
4. Review pending payments.
5. Review petty cash.
6. Review supplier payments.
7. Update finance dashboard.
8. Report important issues to Management.
SECTION 20 - MONTHLY FINANCE ROUTINE
1. Close income register for the month.
2. Close expense register for the month.
3. Review salary payments.
4. Review fixed costs.
5. Prepare profit and loss summary.
6. Prepare cash flow summary.
7. Prepare monthly finance report.
8. Submit finance report to Management.
SECTION 21 - FINANCE CONTROL RULES
1. Every income must be recorded.
2. Every expense must be recorded.
3. Every invoice must have a number.
4. Every receipt must have a number.
5. Payment requests must be approved before payment where required.
6. Petty cash must be reviewed weekly.
7. Salary records must remain confidential.
8. Receipts and invoices must be saved.
9. Monthly finance report must be prepared.
10. Finance tracker must be updated regularly.
SECTION 22 - RESPONSIBILITIES
CEO & Founder
• Final approval of major payments.
• Reviewing monthly finance reports.
• Approving major finance decisions.
• Reviewing company profit and financial direction.
Finance / Office Admin
• Recording income.
• Recording expenses.
• Preparing invoices and receipts.
• Updating petty cash.
• Preparing monthly finance reports.
• Maintaining finance confidentiality.
Sales / Client Relations
• Informing Finance about approved clients.
• Informing Finance when invoices are required.
• Following up on client payments where assigned.
Operations
• Informing Finance about project costs.
• Supporting cost tracking for active projects.
• Confirming project delivery status where payment depends on delivery.
