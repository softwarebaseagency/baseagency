SECTION 1PURPOSE
The purpose of this HR, Attendance & Employee File Tracker is to help Base Agency record, monitor, control, and manage all employee-related information, including employee files, contracts, job descriptions, attendance, fingerprint registration, leave, overtime, probation, performance evaluation, warning records, training records, asset handover, IT access, resignation, termination, and monthly HR reporting.
This tracker shall be used as the official HR tracking and employee control system of Base Agency.
SECTION 2HR TRACKER OBJECTIVES
Base Agency shall use this tracker to achieve the following objectives:
Create a complete employee database.
Track employee file completion.
Track employment agreements and job descriptions.
Track attendance and fingerprint registration.
Track lateness, absence, leave, and overtime.
Track probation and employee confirmation.
Track employee performance evaluation.
Track training and development records.
Track warning and disciplinary records.
Track resignation, termination, and exit clearance.
Prepare monthly HR reports for Management.
Maintain HR confidentiality and document control.

SECTION 29HR CONTROL RULES
The following rules are mandatory:
Every employee must have an employee code.
Every employee must have an employee file.
Every employee must have a job description.
Every employee must sign required HR documents.
Attendance must be recorded daily.
Leave must be approved before absence unless emergency.
Annual leave balance must be updated.
Overtime must be approved before payment.
Probation must be reviewed within 90 days.
Employee evaluations must be documented.
Warnings must be recorded in the employee file.
HR records must remain confidential.
Monthly HR report must be prepared.
The HR tracker must be updated regularly.
