SECTION 1 - PURPOSE
The purpose of this Erbil Target Client List & Outreach Plan is to help Base Agency identify, organize, prioritize, contact, follow up, and convert potential clients in Erbil into active business opportunities.
This document shall be used by Sales, Client Relations, Management, Marketing, and Operations to build a professional client database and execute a structured outreach plan.
SECTION 2 - OUTREACH OBJECTIVES
Build a target client list in Erbil.

Schedule meetings.
Send company profile, proposals, and quotations.
Track follow-ups.
Convert leads into clients.
Create long-term client relationships.
Increase monthly revenue.
Support Base Agency’s market presence in Kurdistan.

SECTION 22 - CLIENT CONVERSION RULES
Every target client must be recorded.
Every contacted client must have a status.
Every interested client must have a follow-up date.
Every meeting must be recorded.
Every proposal must be recorded in CRM.
Every quotation must be recorded in CRM.
Every client approval must be documented.
Every closed deal must be handed over to Finance and Operations.
Lost clients must include reason lost.
Sales activity must be reviewed weekly.
