SECTION 1 - PURPOSE
The purpose of this Company Operating System Dashboard is to provide Base Agency with one central management system to monitor the full company.
This dashboard allows Management to review sales, finance, HR, operations, client experience, quality, risks, document control, approvals, and weekly action items in one structured system.
The dashboard is designed to help Base Agency operate professionally, make better decisions, identify problems early, improve performance, and manage the company with discipline and accountability.
SECTION 2 - SYSTEM OBJECTIVE
The objective of this dashboard is to answer the following management questions:
1. How many clients are we contacting?
2. How many proposals and quotations are active?
3. How much income did we receive?
4. How much did we spend?
5. Are we profitable or losing money?
6. Which projects are active?
7. Which projects are delayed?
8. Are employees attending on time?
9. Are employee files complete?
10. Are clients satisfied?
11. What problems need CEO / Management decision?
12. What actions must be completed this week?

[TABLE]

SECTION 21 - DASHBOARD CONTROL RULES
1. Dashboard data must be updated accurately.
2. No false or estimated data should be entered without note.
3. Every red status must have an action item.
4. Every delayed action must be reviewed weekly.
5. Every pending CEO decision must be recorded.
6. Finance data must remain confidential.
7. HR data must remain confidential.
8. Client information must be protected.
9. The dashboard must be reviewed weekly by Management.
10. Monthly reports must be prepared from dashboard data.
